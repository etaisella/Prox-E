Prox-E

Fine-Grained 3D Shape Editing via Primitive-Based Abstractions

Etai Sella1,2, Hao Phung3, Nitay Amiel4, Or Litany4,5, Or Patashnik1,2, Hadar Averbuch-Elor3
1Tel Aviv University, 2Snap Research, 3Cornell University, 4Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, 5NVIDIA


TL;DR: We introduce Prox-E, a framework for fine-grained 3D shape editing built on primitive-based abstractions. A fuller summary will appear with the camera-ready page.

Abstract

Fine-grained control over 3D shape remains difficult when edits must respect structure, parts, and local detail. Prox-E studies editing through primitive-based abstractions, enabling localized, interpretable manipulation of shape. The full abstract will be posted with the camera-ready PDF.
